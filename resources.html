Resource Library

Curated reading, research, and advocacy opportunities to support your growth. Explore our collection of books, articles, scholarly work, and advocacy resources on women's leadership, workplace equity, and personal empowerment.

Book

Lean In

by Sheryl Sandberg

A powerful exploration of women, work, and the will to lead. Sheryl Sandberg offers practical advice and inspiring insights on how women can overcome barriers to reach their full potential in the workplace and beyond.

Book

Untamed

by Glennon Doyle

A transformative guide to trusting yourself and reclaiming your power. Doyle challenges the expectations society places on women and encourages readers to listen to their inner voice and live authentically.

Book

The Confidence Code

by Katty Kay & Claire Shipman

An exploration of the science and art of self-assurance. Based on groundbreaking research, this book reveals what confidence really is and how women can develop it to succeed in every aspect of their lives.

Article

The Gender Pay Gap: What It Is and Why It Persists

Various Authors

An in-depth overview of wage inequality between genders. This article examines the causes, impacts, and potential solutions to closing the gender pay gap in modern workplaces across industries.

Article

How Young Women Are Redefining Leadership

Various Authors

A look at emerging leadership styles and approaches among young women professionals. Discover how this generation is challenging traditional models and bringing fresh perspectives to leadership roles.

Research

Women in the Workplace 2025

McKinsey & LeanIn.Org

The annual study on the state of women in corporate America. This comprehensive research examines hiring, promotion, attrition, and representation of women across levels and industries.
