Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend?
This conference is designed for high school women (grades 9-12). We welcome students of all backgrounds, interests, and experience levels. All attendees should be prepared to engage respectfully with peers and speakers.
Is there a cost?
Details on registration costs and any scholarship opportunities will be announced closer to the conference date. We are committed to ensuring cost is not a barrier to attendance.
What should I bring?
Bring a notebook and pen for taking notes. Comfortable shoes are recommended as you'll be moving between sessions. A water bottle is helpful to stay hydrated throughout the day.
Will meals be provided?
Yes! Breakfast snacks and lunch will be provided. We will accommodate common dietary restrictions—please note these during registration.
Can I volunteer or speak?
We'd love your involvement! Please reach out through our Connect page to express interest in volunteering, speaking, or leading a workshop.
How do I get there?
Location details and transportation information will be provided once the venue is confirmed. We're exploring options to ensure accessibility for all attendees.
